    THE CHAINSMOKERS IN CONCERT:

    In 2014, up-and-coming EDM duo the Chainsmokers unleashed the "#Selfie" heard round the world. The catchy club anthem was an international sensation, taking the world by storm with its hilarious send-up of self-obsessed internet culture and landing on charts around the globe. With their synth-heavy productions, hilarious stage antics, and party-ready DJ sets, the Chainsmokers have quickly become a must-see live act that fans of adrenaline-filled dance music won't want to miss on tour. 

    BACKGROUND SNAPSHOT:

    The Chainsmokers is comprised of Andrew Taggart and Alex Pall, two New York-based DJs and producers who began creating tracks together after graduating from college. They made their official debut in 2012 with the single "Erase", a sizzling EDM banger featuring budding Indian actress and singer Priyanka Chopra. They followed up in 2013 with "The Rookie", another floor-anthem that continued to build their reputation in dance circles. Their breakthrough success came in 2014 with the release of "#Selfie", a viral sensation that burned up charts around the world and garnered nearly 200 million YouTube views with its hilarious music video. Since then the duo has collaborated with Steve Aoki, remixed artists like Bastille and Chromeo, and thrilled fans around the world with their electrifying live performances.  A potent new force in dance music with big things on the horizon, the Chainsmokers have ticket buyers eagerly awaiting their upcoming tour dates.
